Welcome to Hark! A smarter way of reading!

So what is Hark? Basically, you upload a book, article or document that you’ve been craving to read, but just haven’t found the time to consume.

Hark then takes this text and splits it up into little pieces that are then sent straight to your WhatsApp inbox, all on a schedule that you choose.

This way, you can check off your reading list while on the go, when waiting for an appointment, or any little space of time during your day.
